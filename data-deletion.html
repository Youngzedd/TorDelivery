Delete Your Account

You have the right to request deletion of your Tor Delivery account and all associated personal data. We process all verified requests within 14 business days.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone

Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile and all associated data. Active delivery orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion can be processed. Operational records required for regulatory compliance are retained for 7 years as mandated by Nigerian financial law.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Saved delivery addresses and preferences
  • Contact information (name, phone, email)
  • App usage data and device identifiers

What we must retain

  • Completed delivery records are retained for 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations
  • These records are anonymised from your personal identifiers where possible
  • Transaction data required for dispute resolution

Timeline

Day 0 — Request submitted You receive a confirmation reference
Day 1–2 — Identity verified SMS verification code sent to your number
Within 14 business days Account and personal data permanently deleted

Prefer to delete in-app?

You can also delete your account directly from within the Tor Delivery mobile app. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
